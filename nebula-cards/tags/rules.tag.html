Startup

  • Put one Item card (face-down) on every Treasure planet
  • Put an Enemy card (face-down) on every Enemy planet
  • Put 3 Item cards (face-up) on every Trader planet

Every player:

  • Choose a ship
  • Pull 4 Items from the deck
  • Install desired Items on their Ship
  • Add required amount of Hull and Shield tokens

Play

Players take turns. Every turn a player can perform one actions.

  • Install/Uninstall one Item
  • Travel
  • Attack
  • Travel and Attack
  • Collect treasure
If a player are on a planet with an Enemy, the next turn is that Enemy's turn. They always attack and they are played by the next player.

Combat

Every turn a player or NPC can attack another player or NPC at the same planet. The attacker chooses an Item to attack with. The defender's Shield tokens block the same number of Hull damage. The remaining Hull damage subtract from the defenders Hull. Then the Item's Shield damage subtract from the defenders Shield tokens.

An attack with (Hull:-2, Shield:-1) on a defender with (Hull:5, Shield:1) will leave the defender with (Hull:4, Shield:0).
