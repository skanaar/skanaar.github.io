WHERE CAN YOUR SOLUTION BE IMPLEMENTED?

The locations you type in will be shown with pins on a map on the platform so that potential customers and other interested stakeholders can see where your solution can be implemented. The more precise address you provide the better, but it is OK to only write the name of the cities. We are also particularly interested to know if your solution can be implemented in the cities that are included in our core development team, which is Malmö, Uppsala and Åre/Jämtland region. Some solutions, e.g. those linked to buildings, are easy to add, while others linked to for example mobility, like carpools, can be linked to the most used spot (like a dedicated parking spot). Others, linked to lifestyle choices, will have to be for countries or cities. What is important is that a potential customer, or potential cluster participant, shall know where the solution has been used and where it soon will/could be a reality
